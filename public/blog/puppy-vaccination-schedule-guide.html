Puppy Vaccination Schedule: What Shots Your Puppy Needs and When

July 5, 2026

Bringing home a new puppy? Vaccinations are the single most important thing you will do for their health. Here is the complete schedule.

Core Vaccines

DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parainfluenza, Parvovirus): First dose 6-8 weeks, boosters every 3-4 weeks until 16 weeks. Parvo is deadly — do not skip.

Rabies: Single dose 12-16 weeks. Required by law.

Non-Core Vaccines

Bordetella for daycare. Leptospirosis near wildlife. Lyme in tick areas. Your vet recommends based on location.
